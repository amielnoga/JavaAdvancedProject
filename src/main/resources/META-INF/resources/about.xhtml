About Us

Welcome to Adopet, where three passionate students have teamed up to make the world a better place—one paw at a time!
We’re a trio of tech-loving, pet-obsessed students who believe that every animal deserves a loving home.

Our mission?

To connect adorable, adoptable pets with their future best friends (that’s you!).
With our background in online security, software development, and a shared love for all things furry, we’ve built a safe,
user-friendly platform that makes pet adoption easier, smarter, and more heartwarming than ever.

Why did we start this?

Because we know that a wagging tail or a happy purr can change lives.
And because, let’s be honest, the world could use more paws and less problems.
And because, let's be extra honest, this is our final project in Advanced Java Course.
